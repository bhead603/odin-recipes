Mocha Cookie Bars Recipe

Delicious Mocha cookie bars

The recipe for mocha cookie bars was found on allrecipes and received 5 stars

If you look chocolate and coffee flavors, you will love this cookie bar.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp instant espresso powder
  • 1 tbsp strong brewed coffee
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  • 1 large egg, at room temperature
  • 2 tsps vanilla extract

Topping:

  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Line an 8x8-inch square pan with enough parchment paper to have overhang on all sides.

  2. Stir espresso powder and coffee together in a small bowl until espresso powder is completely dissolved.

  3. Whisk flour, cocoa, cream of tartar, baking soda, salt, and 1/4 tsp cinnamon together in a bowl; set aside.

  4. Beat butter, 1/2 cup white sugar, and brown sugar with an electric mixer together until light and fluffy in a bowl. Beat in espresso mixture, egg, and vanilla until well combined. Stir in flour mixture; mix until dough just begins to form. Press dough firmly and evenly into the prepared pan using lightly floured hands.

  5. Whisk 1 tbsp white sugar and remaining 1/2 tsp cinnaon togther in a small bowl; sprinkle sugar mixture evenly over the dough.

  6. Bake in the preheated oven just until the dough in the center of the pan feels barely set, 20 to 23 minutes.

  7. Allow cookies to cool in the pan for 10 minutes. Use the overhanging parchment to lift cookies out to wire rack; cool completely before cutting into bars.
